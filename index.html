Authority, a customizable arena FPS

By: Xander Productions

Overview

Authority, is an arena shooter that I have been working on for a few days.
I have been designing and polishing this concept for a year.
The goal is to have a working prototype by 4/27/22

Classes

Authority will have 4 main classes. The Assault, Recon, Tank, and Support.
The Assault class will be the average rifleman class that has the most average stats.
The Recon uses sniper rifles and has a lower health and has a smaller size.
The Tank uses shotguns but is much taller and is slower with more health.
The Support uses pistols and is the smallest class in the game with no armor and the fastest speed.

Movement

The movement in Authority will be complex. The goal is to have fast moving fights in the air and on the ground.
The planned movement mechanics include, Sprinting, Sliding, Crouching, and Wall-Running.
I want the game to be fast and exciting while also being challenging.

Weapons

The weapons in Authority will be simple to use but hard to master.
Each weapon will have a different feel using visual tweaks.
All weapons will have recoil and camera shake to give a punchy feel.
Making the weapons feel good to use is my primary objective.

Abilities

Each class in Authority has multiple abilties they can choose from, while only one will be used.
Abilities give the player a better chance to win a fight but also allow you to lose a fight.
Each player will be able to choose an ultimate abilities. Ultimates have a charge that is gained through a match by doing damage.
Ultimate abilities are very strong and can easily help your team win the fight.

Game Mode

The main game mode in Authority is called Multi-Capture.
Multi-Capture works like capture point in every other game but has one small twist.
Multi-Capture has one main capture point and a secondary capture point.
The main capture point scores 2 points per capture tick. While the secondary only scores 1 point.
There are two different secondary point locations on the map. One of the point will unlock and lock every few minutes.
